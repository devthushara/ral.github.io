Antonov An‑225 “Mriya” — The World’s Biggest Aircraft

The Antonov An‑225 “Mriya” (Ukrainian for “Dream”) was a one‑of‑a‑kind strategic airlift cargo aircraft, engineered for outsized payload missions and derived from the An‑124 platform. Its arrival required precise planning, strict ramp discipline, and coordinated, safety‑first ground support.

It gained worldwide attention for its unmatched size and capability — and it became an aviation landmark whenever it visited an airport.

Maiden flight21 December 1988
EnginesSix Progress D‑18T turbofans
Dimensions84 m length • 88.4 m wingspan
Maximum takeoff weight640 tonnes
UniquenessOnly one aircraft was completed

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ground handling and flight support services in Sri Lanka

Is Royal Aviation Lanka approved by CAASL?

Yes, Royal Aviation Lanka is a CAASL (Civil Aviation Authority of Sri Lanka) approved ground handling service provider. We are fully licensed and compliant with all Sri Lankan aviation regulations, ensuring professional standards and regulatory compliance for all operations.

What airports in Sri Lanka do you serve?

We provide comprehensive ground handling and flight support services at three major Sri Lankan airports:

  • Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB/VCBI) - Colombo, primary international gateway
  • Mattala Rajapaksa International Airport (HRI/VCRI) - Southern province, cargo & technical stop operations
  • Ratmalana Airport (RML/VCCS) - General aviation and charter operations
Do you provide 24/7 operations support?

Yes! Our operations center is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year including public holidays. Contact us anytime at +94 71 819 7889 for immediate assistance with permits, ground handling, flight dispatch, or emergency operations support.

How quickly can you arrange a landing permit for Sri Lanka?

Standard landing permits: 48-72 hours processing time
Urgent requests: 24 hours (additional fees may apply)
Diplomatic clearances: 3-5 days
Emergency/Medevac: Expedited processing within hours

We operate 24/7 for emergency coordination and can start the permit application process immediately upon receiving your flight details.

What information do you need to apply for a landing permit?

To apply for a landing permit in Sri Lanka, we need the following information:

  • Aircraft registration and type
  • Operator/airline name and AOC details
  • Proposed arrival and departure dates/times
  • Purpose of flight (charter, positioning, scheduled, Medevac, etc.)
  • Number of passengers and crew
  • Airport of departure and destination
  • Insurance certificate (copy)
  • For diplomatic flights: additional documentation from relevant ministry
Can you arrange overflight permits for Sri Lankan airspace?

Yes, we provide overflight permit services for aircraft transiting through Sri Lankan airspace (FIR). Standard overflight permits are processed within 48-72 hours. We coordinate directly with CAASL and provide you with the official clearance documentation.

What types of aircraft can you handle?

We have experience handling all aircraft categories:

  • Business Jets: Gulfstream, Bombardier, Cessna Citation, Embraer Legacy, Dassault Falcon series
  • Turboprops: King Air, Pilatus PC-12, Quest Kodiak
  • Regional Aircraft: ATR 42/72, Bombardier Dash 8, Embraer E-Jets
  • Narrow-Body: Airbus A320 family, Boeing 737 series
  • Wide-Body: Airbus A330/A340/A350, Boeing 777/787
  • Cargo Aircraft: Boeing 747F, 777F, Antonov An-12/An-124/An-225
  • Military/Government: State aircraft with diplomatic clearances

We handled the world's largest aircraft, the Antonov An-225 "Mriya," at Mattala Airport.

We handle fuel ordering, quality verification, payment arrangements, and invoicing on your behalf. Advance notice (24-48 hours) is recommended for large fuel quantities.

What is the typical turnaround time for a business jet?

Typical turnaround times at Colombo (CMB) and Mattala (HRI):

Turnaround times depend on fuel quantity, passenger count, customs/immigration requirements, and any special services requested. We coordinate all aspects to minimize ground time.
